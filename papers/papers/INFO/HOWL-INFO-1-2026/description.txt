
Multi-Dimensional Information Indexing

A Framework for Context-Preserving Knowledge Management

Traditional information management treats knowledge as static truth claims that must be accepted or rejected. This paper documents an alternative framework observed in expert practice: multi-dimensional indexing that preserves source, temporal, contextual, and intentional metadata alongside content.
