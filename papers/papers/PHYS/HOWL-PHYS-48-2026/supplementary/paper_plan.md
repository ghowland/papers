### THE THESIS

Every QED coefficient from one loop through three loops decomposes entirely into two categories: spherical geometry (β², β⁴ — from angular integrations producing π powers) and number theory (β⁰ — rationals, ζ values, polylogarithms). At four loops, a third category appears for the first time: geometric constants that carry no π. The Laporta integrals are β⁰ by the MATH-11 classification (24/24 PSLQ null against π), but they are NOT number-theoretic (24/24 PSLQ null against ζ, Li, MZV). They are geometric constants from a non-spherical topology — the first evidence that QED contains toroidal geometry.

### THE STAIRCASE

**Stair 1: MATH-11 showed β = π/4 is the L1/L2 conversion on spherical geometry.** Every factor of π in a QED coefficient traces to an angular integration over a circular/spherical subspace of loop momentum. Counting π powers counts spherical angular integrations. β² = one angular integration. β⁴ = two. The framework classifies every term.

**Stair 2: The classification reveals a progression from A₁ to A₃.** A₁ = ½: pure β⁰, one term, no geometry. A₂: β⁰ (46.6%) and β² (53.4%), four terms, 90.4% cancellation. A₃: β⁰ (51.3%), β² (24.7%), β⁴ (24.0%), nine terms, 99.5% cancellation. Each loop adds a β power and tightens the cancellation. The geometric fraction slowly decreases from 53% to 49%.

**Stair 3: At four loops, the pattern breaks.** A₄ contains the six Laporta integrals. PSLQ proves they have no π content (β⁰). PSLQ also proves they are not ζ, not Li, not MZV, not alternating Euler sums. They are β⁰ but not number-theoretic. This is a new subcategory that doesn't exist at loops 1-3.

**Stair 4: The new subcategory is toroidal geometry.** The elliptic magnitude scan shows all six integrals match K(k)×E(k) combinations to 0.006% or better. The inter-integral ratios near simple fractions (−40/3, −24/7, 13/7) suggest partial algebraic structure. The topologies 81 and 83 have distinct ratio signatures (elongated vs compact) consistent with different torus aspect ratios.

**Stair 5: The muon proves the toroidal sector scales with mass.** The mass-dependent four-loop corrections scale as (m_μ/m_e)² = 42,753. For the electron, the toroidal sector is 0.05% of the universal. For the muon, it's 2304%. The electron sees spherical geometry. The muon sees toroidal geometry. Same diagrams, different probe mass, different dominant sector.

**Stair 6: The dual geometry is universal.** Every soliton has spherical boundaries (gravity, thermal) and toroidal boundaries (magnetic, rotational). The proton has a spherical confinement boundary and toroidal gluon flux tubes. The Earth has spherical atmospheric shells and toroidal Van Allen belts. QED at four loops has spherical angular integrals producing π and toroidal topologies producing the Laporta constants. The pattern repeats from the quantum vacuum to the galaxy.
