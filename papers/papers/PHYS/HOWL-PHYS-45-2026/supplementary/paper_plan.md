### THE STAIRCASE

This paper introduces the confinement boundary to a reader who has followed PHYS-41 through PHYS-44. Each stair builds on the one before.

**Stair 1: The hierarchy exists and has readings.** PHYS-41 established that the soliton hierarchy has nested boundaries from Planck to cosmos, and that physical constants are readings at each depth. PHYS-42 confirmed this: one derivation function reproduced GR time dilation across 18 orders of magnitude. The readings work. The hierarchy is real.

**Stair 2: The readings decompose into space and time.** PHYS-44 separated the GR dilation formula into two components. The reading (D) is spatial structure — computable at frozen time. The tick (K) is the Planck counting process — what makes things happen. Classification: 10 pure D, 1 pure K, 4 mixed, 3 structural. The frozen scan covers 89%. Space carries the structure. Time carries the actuality.

**Stair 3: The boundaries have properties, not just readings.** This is where PHYS-45 begins. PHYS-41 through PHYS-44 computed readings AT boundaries — the gravitational potential at Earth's surface, the coupling values at M_Z, the clock rates at different depths. But the boundaries themselves were not characterized. What is a boundary? Where does it sit? How thick is it? What determines its position?

**Stair 4: The confinement boundary answers all four questions.** A boundary is where a coupling reaches criticality. It sits at the energy scale Λ where α diverges (or reaches a critical value). Its thickness is 1/|b|, determined by the beta coefficient. Its position is set by the beta coefficient and the coupling value at a reference scale. Everything follows from exact Fractions.

**Stair 5: The proton is 99% boundary.** The proton's inertia is 938.3 MeV. The quarks inside contribute 9 MeV. The remaining 929 MeV is the energy stored in the confinement field configuration — the boundary itself. When we measure the proton mass, we measure the boundary. The proton is not a bag of quarks. It is a soliton whose inertia IS its boundary energy.

**Stair 6: The CD repositions the boundary.** The Cabibbo Doublet shifts b₃ from −7 to −20/3. This shifts Λ_QCD by 2%. The boundary moves by an exact Fraction. The proton mass shifts by 2%. The pion mass shifts. The nuclear force range shifts. Everything downstream of the confinement boundary inherits the CD's fingerprint — an exact Fraction propagated through 5 decades of energy.

**Stair 7: Every boundary in the hierarchy has this structure.** The confinement boundary is the prototype. The electroweak boundary, the atomic boundary, the nuclear boundary, the gravitational boundaries — each is a threshold where a coupling reaches criticality, positioned by exact Fractions, hosting solitons inside. The hierarchy is not just organized by integers. It is constructed by them. The boundaries are where they are because the beta coefficients are what they are.
